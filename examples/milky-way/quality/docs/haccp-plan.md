# HACCP plan — Stjerneholmen plant

Critical control points (excerpt):

| CCP | Hazard | Limit | Monitoring |
|---|---|---|---|
| CCP-1 pasteurisation | pathogen survival | 72°C / 15s minimum | continuous, logged |
| CCP-2 cold chain | pathogen growth | ≤4°C storage | sensor per chamber, hourly |
| CCP-3 allergen changeover | cross-contact | validated clean between lines | swab per changeover |

Corrective actions and verification records are retained 5 years and are
the first thing an auditor asks for.
