# Food safety authority audit — preparation checklist

- Pull 12 months of CCP monitoring logs (pasteurisation, cold chain,
  allergen changeover).
- Verify corrective-action records are signed and closed.
- Current HACCP plan version printed and on the table.
- Training records for everyone on the production floor that day.
- The quality manager hosts; the plant manager opens and closes.

Do not volunteer draft documents or future-dated procedures — answer what
is asked from what is in force.
