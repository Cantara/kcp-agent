# The Melkeveien documentation estate

Eight domains publish knowledge under this hub. Start here; the federation
block routes you onward:

- **Integration Platform** (`platform`) — the live integration catalog, API
  schemas, architecture decisions, on-call runbooks. Production and staging.
- **Dev Mirror** (`dev-mirror`) — sandbox endpoints and mock data. Dev only.
- **Quality & Food Safety** (`quality`) — HACCP, audit preparation, incoming
  regulation.
- **R&D** (`recipes`) — formulations and pilot results. Attested agents only.
- **People** (`people`) — onboarding and HR processes.
- **Brand & Communications** (`brand`) — guidelines and the press kit.
- **Sustainability** (`esg`) — CSRD reporting, emissions data.
- **Orion Business Systems** (`vendor`) — external ERP documentation, behind
  the vendor portal.
