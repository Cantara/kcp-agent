# FC-2026-017 — Quaymaster Broker: active exploitation (interim mitigation)

*(Fictional example advisory.)*

**Status:** active exploitation observed against energy and logistics
sectors. No vendor patch available at time of publication.

**Interim mitigation:** disable the Quaymaster Broker management listener
(`management.listener.enabled=false`) or firewall its port to management
hosts only. Restart is required for the setting to take effect.

**Detection:** review management-listener access logs for unauthenticated
POST requests preceding broker child-process spawns.

This advisory is superseded by FC-2026-017 rev2 once the vendor patch is
available.
