# Family itineraries around Fjordholm

Three villages, one ferry, no car needed. Sandvik for the shallow beach and
the boat museum; Midholm for the sea-eagle viewpoint (stroller-friendly
boardwalk); Otterby for the fish market and the rainy-day science barn.

Travel times: Sandvik–Midholm 25 min by ferry, Midholm–Otterby 40 min.
Plan around the timetable — the last evening sailing is the one families miss.
