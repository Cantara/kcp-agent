# Allergen-aware dining around Fjordholm

Certified nut-free kitchens (audited annually, certificates on file):

- **The Boathouse Kitchen**, Sandvik — nut-free kitchen, full vegan menu,
  allergen matrix at the counter and in the booking API.
- **Lighthouse Café**, Otterby — nut-free kitchen, vegan options daily.

**The Wharf Grill**, Midholm serves nuts in shared fryers — great reviews,
not certified. The certificate, not the review score, is the safety signal.
